BREWED TO GIVE YOU A KICK

The Dandy Brewing Company is Calgary's original small brewery. Although it has grown a little, it's still small at heart. We produce inspired ales, with lots of care, in small batches. Our unique approach to brewing and keen eye to detail and fosterscreativity in both our recipes and beer styles.On site, we are pleased to offer a unique brewery experience, featuring a full service tasting room with an exquisite menu of fine dining drinking food, a curated selection of cocktails, glass pours of selected wines, and 20 taps of Dandy beers. We share a passion for not only brewing, but the people, process, experiences, and personal touches that make the real ale experience so special.

JOIN THE FAMILY OF FLAVOURS

One thing which makes us different from other breweries is the different flavours that we provide to our customers. We have gained the skill to mold any flavours into beers and ales. Our most famous beers are Purple Haze, Light Lime, Strawberry and so many more.

Promoting Beer Culture

We are not only known for how to make the best beers and ales in Alberta, we are also known to promote beer culture through many ways. Dandy Brewing Co endorses many artist and we encourage artists to collaborate with dandies in events. To know more about this program.

The Tasting Rooms rooms

We are running a special program called The Tasting Rooms to give you a better idea of what we make. We provide you access to 20 taps of Dandy Beers(maybe a few rad guest taps too), a curated cocktail list featuring local spirit, and a daily selection of top quality wines available by the glass.
